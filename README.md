AI Career Coach

Product Description

AI Career Coach is an AI-powered career assistant designed primarily for university students and new graduates. The application analyzes uploaded CVs and provides personalized career guidance using artificial intelligence.

Users receive a comprehensive CV evaluation, identify missing skills, get personalized career roadmaps, practice interviews through AI-generated questions, and optimize their LinkedIn profiles to increase their employability.

Product Features
 CV Upload (PDF / DOCX)
 AI-powered CV Analysis
 CV Score (Overall & Category-based)
 Strengths & Weaknesses Detection
 Target Role Based Evaluation
 Personalized Career Roadmap
 Actionable Improvement Suggestions
 AI Interview Simulation
 Interview Feedback
 LinkedIn Profile Optimization
 Previous Analysis History
